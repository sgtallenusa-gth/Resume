# Case Study: Dell Technical Support Foundation

## Role

Level I / Level II Technical Support Engineer

## Dates

2001 to 2004

## Context

My professional technology career began in customer-facing technical support, troubleshooting Dell hardware and operating system issues for users with varying levels of technical experience.

## Growth

I advanced from Level I to Level II support and developed the ability to handle difficult customers, escalated technical problems, and high-pressure conversations.

## Certifications

- Dell Certified Systems Expert (DCSE)
- Dell Black Belt

## Career Impact

This role built the foundation for my communication style. It taught me that solving the technical problem is only part of the job. The rest is helping people stay confident, informed, and calm while the problem is being solved.

That skill has carried forward into sales engineering, executive briefings, outage response, team leadership, and customer-facing solution architecture.

# Case Study: Charter / Spectrum Cloud Deployment Engineering

## Role

Systems Engineer III, Deployment Team

## Dates

2016

## Context

At Charter / Spectrum, I worked as part of a deployment team responsible for building and maintaining the microservice infrastructure that supported customer-facing services.

These services included video, music, telephony, and related platform components.

## Technologies

- AWS
- Ansible
- Microservices
- Automated deployment workflows
- Cloud platform operations

## Career Impact

Although this was a shorter assignment, it was a powerful learning experience. It exposed me to cloud-native infrastructure, automation at scale, and the idea that infrastructure should be treated as repeatable software-driven systems rather than one-off server builds.

Many of the automation and deployment principles I later applied at ADP and Chaparral were influenced by this experience.
